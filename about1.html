SHIPPING AND HANDLING

All orders will be dispatched within 1-2 business days. All orders are also tracked. Breathe Divinity does not take responsibility for any parcels that go missing beyond being shipped. We are not responsible for delays, orders sent to incorrect addresses, lost or stolen.

TRACK YOUR ORDER

Download SHOP app for free on the app store to track your order or use the tracking number provided VIA email confirmation to manually track it on the couriers (USPS,FEDEX,UPS) website.

******PRE-ORDERS*******

A pre-order is an item available to order but not manufactured yet. Pre-orders will often happen on our site if an item has absurdly high demand. If the item you are ordering is listed as a pre-order, please ALLOW 2-4 WEEKS for the package to ship from site closure. Please do not order a pre-order item if you are not willing to wait the estimated time. All sales are final on pre-orders unless your item is damaged upon receiving.
